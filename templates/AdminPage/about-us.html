Our Mission

Our Room Booking System is designed to streamline room reservations for students and staff at RUPP. We aim to provide an efficient, user-friendly platform that saves time and reduces administrative overhead.

We are students from Department of Data Science and Engineering, RUPP, passionate developers committed to improving university operations through technology.

Our Vision

To create a seamless, transparent, and reliable room booking experience that enhances productivity and reduces conflicts within the university environment.

Making room booking simple, accessible, and efficient for everyone at RUPP.

Time Saving

Automated booking processes that save time for both students and administrators

Easy Scheduling

Accessible anytime, anywhere with our responsive web interface

Live room availability updates to prevent double-bookings
