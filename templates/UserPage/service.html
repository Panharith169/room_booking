Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room?

To book a room, navigate to the Booking page, select your preferred room type, date, and time. Fill in the required information and submit your booking request. You'll receive a confirmation email once approved.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Go to your Booked page and select the booking you want to change.

What are the room booking policies?

Rooms must be booked at least 2 hours in advance. Maximum booking duration is 4 hours. Late cancellations may result in booking restrictions. Please respect the facilities and clean up after use.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the Settings page and select "Change Password". You'll need to enter your current password and set a new one. Make sure your new password meets the security requirements.
